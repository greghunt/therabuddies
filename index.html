What are Power Buddies?

Power Buddies are adorable plush companions that offer gentle support for both children and adults with ADHD, ASD, SPD, stress or trauma through their healthful therapeutic qualities. Designed warmly to alleviate users of daily tensions and distraction, Power Buddies promote feelings of stability, relaxation and happiness.
